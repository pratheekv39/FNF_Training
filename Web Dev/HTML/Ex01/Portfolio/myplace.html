My Hometown: Hassan

I grew up in Hassan, a beautiful city located in the heart of Karnataka. Known for its rich history, serene landscapes, and architectural marvels, Hassan holds a special place in my heart. It is a gateway to some of the most iconic temples and heritage sites in South India, including the famous Chennakeshava Temple in Belur.

What Makes Hassan Special

  1. Home to ancient Hoysala architecture like Belur and Halebidu temples
  2. Pleasant weather and lush green surroundings
  3. Rich cultural heritage and traditional festivals
  4. Peaceful lifestyle with a strong sense of community
